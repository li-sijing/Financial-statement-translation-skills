## 二、硬要求（版本(3)定义，优先级最高）

| 账面价值 | **Book value** | — |
| 永续债 | **Perpetual bonds** | Sustainable debt |

### 标题风格硬要求
- 英文只**首词首字母大写**（sentence case）
  - 正确: `Book balance`、`Provision for bad debts`
  - 错误: `Book Balance`、`Provision For Bad Debts`
- 固定缩写保持大写: **RMB、CAS、FVOCI、FVTPL**
- 公司名称全文统一，不可随意改写
- 表格术语优先于正文术语

### 处理原则硬要求
- 标题序号不能机械照抄（错号/跳号/重复号需主动纠正）
- 参考文档不是机械模板，需结合上下文做专业判断
- 中式英语、生硬搭配应优先修正为正式财报英文

## 五、固定句式（可直接复用）

### 持续经营
> The financial statements of the Company are prepared on a going concern basis.

### 编制基础
> Based on actual transactions and events, the statements are prepared in accordance with the Accounting Standards for Business Enterprises.

### 现金等价物定义
> Cash equivalents refer to investments held by the Company that are short-term, highly liquid, readily convertible to known amounts of cash, and subject to an insignificant risk of changes in value.

### 金融资产分类
> Financial assets are classified at initial recognition based on the Company's business model for managing financial assets and the contractual cash flow characteristics of the financial assets.

### 坏账准备（应收款项）
> For receivables formed by transactions regulated by the Revenue Standard (regardless of whether they contain a significant financing component), the Company measures the loss allowance at an amount equal to the lifetime expected credit losses.

### 存货
> Inventories are measured at the lower of cost and net realizable value.

### 收入确认
> The Company recognizes revenue when the customer obtains control of the relevant goods, provided that the contract between the Company and the customer meets the following conditions simultaneously.

### 租赁（承租人）
> The Company as a lessee recognizes the right to use the leased asset as a right-of-use asset and recognizes the present value of the unpaid lease payments as a lease liability.

### 政府补助
> Government grants related to assets are recognized as deferred income and are included in profit or loss for the current period or offset against the carrying amount of the relevant assets in installments using a reasonable and systematic method over the useful life of the relevant assets.

### 递延所得税
> Deferred tax assets are recognized to the extent that it is probable that taxable income will be available against which the deductible temporary differences can be utilized.

### 承诺事项
> The Company has no significant commitments requiring disclosure.

### 或有事项
> As of the balance sheet date, there were no significant contingencies requiring disclosure.

### 受限资金
> Except for the funds mentioned above, the Company has no funds that are pledged, mortgage-backed, frozen, or deposited overseas with restrictions on repatriation.

- **原版**: `6.65 Income from asset disposal`

### Conflict 5: "所得税费用" → Income tax expense (单数) vs Income tax expenses (复数)
- **版本(3)**: Income tax expense

### Conflict 6: "信用减值损失" → Credit impairment loss (单数) vs Credit impairment losses (复数)
